# Sketch Manifest

## Design Direction
InfoTriage **SP-COP** — the SAB reimagined as an interactive operating-picture canvas (replacing World
Monitor, which R5 dropped). Dark intel-console aesthetic, fully local, over the operator's own CCIR/CNR-
scored corpus. The organizing principle is **multiple modes, no limits — LOOK / HEADLINES / FOCUS** across
two axes: **known↔unknown** (directed CCIR vs serendipitous discovery) and **ambient↔focused** (lean-back
glance vs lean-forward deep-dive). Topics are launchpads (dig-in → RAG/Wiki-LLM, spin-up POC); leads can be
parked for later. Substrate: split geo + entity-network (R3 entity_links) + shared timeline scrubber.

## Reference Points
World Monitor (globe view, floating pickers — concept only) · Palantir Gotham (object-link Graph Canvas,
linked views, timeline brushing/playback, neighborhood spotlight) · IBM i2 Analyst's Notebook (geo+network+
temporal) · Maltego (entity transforms = dig-in) · InfraNodus (gap/bridge discovery, AI questions) ·
Sensecape (LLM multilevel sensemaking) · Shneiderman semantic-zoom mantra · calm technology (ambient mode).
