| T-02-03 | Information Disclosure | INFOTRIAGE_PG_DSN handling | high | mitigate | DSN read only from the environment by the caller (D-03); never hard-coded or logged. The dev DSN used in tests targets the dev-only compose password; `.env` stays gitignored (NF-6). |
