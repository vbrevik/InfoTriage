Show local models can synthesize a coherent, cited wiki page from the corpus. Produce (a) one
standing entity/topic wiki page from >=5 real items with bracketed citations to source ids and (b)
one on-demand article, both on qwen36. This resolves R4. Wiki-LLM production (standing auto-wiki +
on-demand) is Phase 10 (Deferred); the spike only proves feasibility.

(Phase 8 entity resolution -> Phase 10 Wiki-LLM): the on-demand article uses R3 entity_links to gather
cross-language mentions of the topic entity (D-03 reuse of the same corpus).
Output: wiki synthesis script + two saved samples + durable R4 verdict.
</objective>
