<objective>
Build apps/triage/worker.py — the event-driven triage container entry point. It consumes
item.ingested, dedups with mE5-large embeddings, scores non-duplicates against ccir.md with
qwen36, persists enrichment, and publishes verdict.ready, alongside a stdlib /health server.

Implements D-01 (worker.py entry; triage_score preserved as pure helper), D-03 (asyncio.gather
of consumer + health server under asyncio.run), and D-04 (stdlib asyncio.start_server health,
liveness only). Consumes the 05-01 store methods and the 05-02 consume()/hot-read scorer.

Purpose: this is the core of the phase — the proven scorer behind the bus.

Output: worker.py with get_embedding, map_cnr, map_bucket, score clamp, process_item core,
on_message handler, run_consumer, run_health_server, main; worker behavior tests; health test.
</objective>
